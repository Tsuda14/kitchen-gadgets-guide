Consider how often you will actually use it before deciding on a budget.

How do I maintain my Instant Pot for optimal performance?

Regular cleaning is the single most important maintenance step. Follow the manufacturer's recommended cleaning schedule, replace filters or consumables on time, and address any unusual noises or performance changes promptly. A well-maintained Instant Pot can easily last years beyond a neglected one.
